Specs + Features

The Project

Gamer Approved!

Robot Reviews is a custom trained GPT-2 model that learned from two different Kaggle data sets that have over 6 million steam reviews!

Interactive

Our ai will produce a review of a positive or negative review of a game after the user inputs a title, genre, and rating type. The ai should also be able to produce a review on a made-up game!

Timeline

The Review Robot was completed by teenagers over the course of three weeks this summer!

Extensions

We might also have out Robot review bot produce multiple reviews at once, include extra info about the game, and give the user the ability to choose how long the review should be!

Our Dataset

Because our dataset has over 6 million reviews inside of it we can get reviews that make sense as output. However, this does cause the training time to take longer. But the extra time it takes to wait for the training is worth the result.
Our dataset has many different genres of game reviews in it ranging from fps games, mmo games, racing games, multiplayer games, etc. This allows us to get a large variety of results when we generate a review.

Our Model

We trained this AI with a GPT-Neo model. We chose this over other models like GPT-2 because it would produce better results. However, this does come with the downside of taking longer to train.
The model was trained on the Pile, which is a large dataset created by EleutherAI, a group of researchers that work to open-source AI. It was trained on pile with 380 billion tokens over 362,000 steps.
